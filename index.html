Near-death experiences: what does the science actually say?

The AWARE II study followed 567 cardiac arrest patients. Survivors reported powerful, lucid experiences distinct from hallucinations or dreams. EEG recordings showed unexpected brain electrical activity long into CPR. The data is real. The explanation is still open.
